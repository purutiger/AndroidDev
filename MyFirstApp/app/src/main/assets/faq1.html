FAQ's for Manual Connection Control

Q: What is Manual Control?
A: This section allows you to toggle ON/OF various connections such as Data (2g/3g/4g), Wifi, Bluetooth and NFC
Apart from this, t also aloows you to toggle Sync. Sync is android service which when started syncs your emals, news providers, phots, google+, facebook updates ets.


Q: Why doesnt NFC toggle directly? Why does it take me to the NFC settings page?
A: Android doesnt allow us to access the NFC APIs directly to toggle it.
